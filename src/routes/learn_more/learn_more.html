LEARN MORE ABOUT HANDMADE PIXEL

Handmade Pixel is a platform designed to provide a dynamic learning experience in computer graphics. The project emphasizes interactive teaching methods, enabling users to visualize and explore complex graphics concepts in an engaging manner.

What Makes Handmade Pixel Unique?

  • Interactive Learning: Users can interact with graphical windows to better understand concepts in real time, enhancing comprehension and engagement.
  • Beginner-Friendly: Lessons are crafted to make advanced topics accessible to learners of all levels, removing the intimidation barrier for those new to computer graphics.
  • Hands-On Experience: By integrating coding with visualization, users can gain practical knowledge and see the immediate impact of their work.

Features

  • Comprehensive Lessons: Each lesson is divided into manageable components, featuring written explanations, interactive demos, and project-based learning.
  • Accounts & Progress Tracking: Users can create accounts to save lessons, track their progress, and achieve milestones.
  • Interactive Playground: A dedicated space where users can experiment with tools and concepts introduced in the lessons.

Our Mission

Handmade Pixel aims to bridge the gap between creativity and computer science by making computer graphics approachable and inspiring. By demystifying the subject through interactive methods, we hope to encourage learners to see the artistic potential of computer graphics and unleash their creativity.

Target Audience

Whether you're a beginner exploring graphics for the first time or an experienced programmer looking for inspiration, Handmade Pixel offers resources to meet your needs. The platform is designed for:

  • Game developers and visual effects artists.
  • Students interested in computer science and graphics.
  • Educators seeking innovative ways to teach complex topics.

Join Us

Dive into the world of computer graphics and unlock endless possibilities. Handmade Pixel is here to guide you every step of the way, making learning both fun and impactful.
