UNIT 1: INTRODUCTION TO METABALLS & MARCHING SQUARES

In this unit, you will learn about metaballs: organic n-dimensional isosurfaces. Metaballs are notable for their ability to blend together after being in close proximity, making a single contiguous figure. You will learn about the implementation of metaballs using the Marching Squares algorithm. Throughout this unit, you will:

  • Understand the mathematical and graphical concepts behind metaballs.
  • Visualize and manipulate metaballs in 2D space.
  • Implement and optimize the Marching Squares algorithm to render metaballs efficiently.
  • Understand real-world applications of metaballs

At the end of this unit, you'll have the skills needed to fully understand metaballs and implement them in your own projects.
